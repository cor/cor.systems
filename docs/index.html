About

I'm a developer who is specialised in designing and building web applications using Vue.js, but likes doing (almost) every kind of programming. I enjoy working on creative solutions to complex problems, learning about new technologies and working together with a great team.

My interest in programming started when I was 11 years old. My dad taught me how to write basic command line programs in C, and since then I've worked on a lot of different programming projects, using numerous languages, frameworks and technologies. I have worked professionally as a front end web developer and designer for multiple clients, and continue to do so. I also occasionally deliver tech support to one person businesses, friends, and my family.

I'm currently in my second year of studying Computer Science and Game Development at the Utrecht University. While working on a lot of programming projects, I also managed to get my high school degree in 2018. I did VWO NT with extra Mathematics, Computer Science and Art as my chosen subjects. I graduated with a 9 for Mathematics B, Mathematics D and Computer Science, and an 8 for Physics and English.
